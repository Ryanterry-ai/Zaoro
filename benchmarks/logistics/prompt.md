# Logistics Benchmark Prompt

Build a logistics and fleet management platform for trucking companies and freight brokers. Features: load board, dispatch management, fleet tracking (GPS/ELD), driver management, HOS compliance, IFTA reporting, maintenance tracking, fuel management, document management, rate management, quoting, invoicing, factoring integration, customer portal, driver mobile app, analytics. Target: small to mid-size carriers and brokers.

## Key Requirements

### Load Management
- Load board with available loads
- Load creation and dispatch
- Load tracking and status updates
- Load documents

### Fleet Management
- Vehicle and trailer management
- GPS tracking integration
- ELD compliance
- Driver management

### Compliance
- Hours of Service (HOS) logging
- IFTA reporting
- DVIR tracking
- Inspection records

### Operations
- Fuel management
- Maintenance scheduling
- Rate quoting
- Invoicing and payments

### Customer Portal
- Real-time tracking
- Document access
- Rate requests
- Invoice viewing
