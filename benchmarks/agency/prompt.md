# Agency Benchmark Prompt

Build an agency management platform for digital marketing, design, and development agencies. Features: project management, resource planning, time tracking, client portal, proposals/contracts, invoicing, retainers, capacity planning, profitability analysis, file sharing, feedback/approvals, team collaboration, reporting, CRM, pipeline, lead tracking, project templates, asset library, brand guidelines, white-label reports. Target: agencies 5-200 people.

## Key Requirements

### Project Management
- Project creation
- Task management
- Timeline tracking
- Project templates

### Resource Planning
- Capacity planning
- Resource allocation
- Utilization tracking
- Forecasting

### Time & Billing
- Time tracking
- Time approvals
- Invoicing
- Retainer management

### Client Communication
- Client portal
- File sharing
- Feedback/approvals
- Proposals and contracts

### Business Intelligence
- Profitability reports
- Utilization reports
- Revenue reports
- Lead pipeline
