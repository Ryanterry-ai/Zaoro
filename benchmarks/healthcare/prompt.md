# Healthcare Benchmark Prompt

Build a HIPAA-compliant practice management system for multi-specialty clinics. Features: patient registration, scheduling (multi-provider, multi-location), EHR/clinical notes, e-prescribing, billing/claims, insurance verification, patient portal, telehealth, lab integration, reporting, inventory, staff management. Target: 5-50 provider practices.

## Key Requirements

### Patient Management
- Patient registration and demographics
- Insurance verification
- Medical history and records
- Patient portal access

### Scheduling
- Multi-provider calendar
- Multi-location support
- Appointment types
- Waitlist management

### Clinical
- EHR/clinical notes
- E-prescribing integration
- Lab order and results
- Document management

### Billing
- Claims submission
- Payment processing
- Insurance management
- Reporting

### Technical
- HIPAA compliance
- Audit logging
- Role-based access
- API integrations
