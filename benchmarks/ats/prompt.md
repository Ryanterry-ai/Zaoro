# ATS Benchmark Prompt

Build a modern ATS for recruiting teams and agencies. Features: job posting (multi-board), candidate sourcing, resume parsing, pipeline management, interview scheduling, collaborative hiring, offer management, onboarding, reporting, EEO/OFCCP compliance, career site, candidate portal, email integration, calendar sync, AI matching, talent pools, referral program. Target: in-house recruiting teams and staffing agencies.

## Key Requirements

### Job Posting
- Create and manage jobs
- Multi-board posting (Indeed, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Career site hosting
- Job application forms

### Candidate Management
- Candidate database
- Resume parsing
- Pipeline management
- Talent pools

### Hiring Process
- Interview scheduling
- Scorecards and evaluations
- Offer management
- Onboarding tasks

### Collaboration
- Interviewer assignments
- Feedback collection
- Candidate rating
- Team notes

### Compliance
- EEO tracking
- OFCCP reporting
- Audit logs
- Data retention
