# Fitness Benchmark Prompt

Build a fitness studio management platform for gyms, yoga studios, CrossFit boxes, and personal trainers. Features: class scheduling, booking, membership management, recurring billing, check-in (app, kiosk, QR), waitlists, packages/passes, retail/POS, staff management, payroll, workout tracking, progress photos, nutrition, challenges, mobile app (member + coach), branded app, marketing automation, reporting. Target: boutique studios to multi-location gyms.

## Key Requirements

### Class Management
- Class scheduling
- Instructor management
- Waitlist handling
- Class types and categories

### Membership
- Membership plans
- Recurring billing
- Packages and passes
- Freeze/pause options

### Operations
- Check-in (kiosk, QR, app)
- Retail POS
- Staff scheduling
- Payroll processing

### Member Experience
- Workout tracking
- Progress photos
- Nutrition tracking
- Challenges and gamification

### Business
- Marketing automation
- Reporting and analytics
- Mobile apps (member/coach)
- Branded app white-label
