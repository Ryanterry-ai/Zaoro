# Accounting Benchmark Prompt

Build a practice management platform for accounting firms. Features: client management, engagement letters, time & billing, project management, document management, secure file sharing, tax preparation workflow, audit workflow, payroll services, client portal, e-signature, CPE tracking, compliance calendar, reporting, multi-entity consolidation, integration with QuickBooks/Xero/tax software. Target: CPA firms 5-200 staff.

## Key Requirements

### Client Management
- Client database
- Contact management
- Engagement letters
- Client portal

### Time & Billing
- Time entries
- Expense tracking
- Invoicing
- Payment tracking

### Document Management
- Secure file storage
- Sharing and permissions
- Document templates
- Version control

### Tax & Audit
- Tax return workflow
- Audit engagement
- Workpapers
- Compliance tracking

### Reporting
- Realization reports
- Aging reports
- Client profitability
- Staff utilization
