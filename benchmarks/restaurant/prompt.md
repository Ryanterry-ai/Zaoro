# Restaurant Benchmark Prompt

Build a comprehensive restaurant management platform. Features: POS (tableside, kiosk, online), menu management, inventory (recipe costing, waste tracking), staff scheduling, labor costing, reservations, waitlist, online ordering, delivery integration, loyalty/gift cards, kitchen display system, reporting, multi-location, franchise support, accounting integration. Target: independent restaurants to multi-unit chains.

## Key Requirements

### POS System
- Tableside ordering
- Kiosk mode
- Online ordering
- Quick service mode

### Menu Management
- Menu item CRUD
- Modifiers and variants
- Recipe costing
- Menu engineering

### Inventory
- Recipe-based inventory
- Waste tracking
- Par levels
- Purchase orders

### Staff
- Scheduling
- Time tracking
- Labor costing
- Payroll integration

### Operations
- Reservations
- Waitlist management
- Delivery integration
- Kitchen display system

### Business
- Reporting and analytics
- Multi-location support
- Franchise management
- Accounting integration
