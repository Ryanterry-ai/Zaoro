# HRMS Benchmark Prompt

Build a modern HRMS for mid-market companies. Features: employee database, onboarding/offboarding, time & attendance, leave management, payroll integration, benefits administration, performance reviews, goals/OKRs, learning management, compensation planning, org charts, document management, analytics, employee self-service, mobile app, compliance tracking, ATS integration. Target: 50-2000 employee companies.

## Key Requirements

### Employee Management
- Employee database
- Onboarding workflows
- Offboarding processes
- Document management

### Time & Attendance
- Time tracking
- Leave management
- Scheduling
- Timesheet approval

### Compensation
- Payroll integration
- Benefits administration
- Compensation planning
- Salary history

### Performance
- Performance reviews
- Goals/OKRs
- Learning management
- Competency tracking

### Analytics
- Headcount reporting
- Turnover analysis
- Compensation analytics
- Compliance reports

### Employee Self-Service
- Profile management
- Time-off requests
- Pay stubs
- Benefits enrollment
