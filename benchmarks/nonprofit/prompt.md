# Nonprofit Benchmark Prompt

Build a comprehensive nonprofit management platform. Features: donor management, fundraising (campaigns, peer-to-peer, events, recurring), grant management, volunteer management, program management, case management, membership, advocacy, email marketing, reporting (990, impact), accounting integration, compliance, board management, planned giving, corporate partnerships. Target: small to large nonprofits (501c3, 501c4, etc.).

## Key Requirements

### Donor Management
- Donor database
- Giving history
- Donor segmentation
- Stewardship tracking

### Fundraising
- Campaign management
- Peer-to-peer fundraising
- Event fundraising
- Recurring giving

### Volunteer Management
- Volunteer opportunities
- Shift scheduling
- Hour tracking
- Volunteer portal

### Program Management
- Program tracking
- Outcomes measurement
- Case management
- Service delivery

### Reporting
- 990 reporting
- Impact metrics
- Donor reports
- Financial reports
