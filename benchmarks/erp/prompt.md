# ERP Benchmark Prompt

Build a comprehensive ERP system for mid-market manufacturers. Modules: finance (GL, AP, AR, fixed assets), inventory (multi-warehouse, lot/serial tracking), procurement (PO, vendor management), manufacturing (BOM, work orders, routing), sales (quotes, orders, fulfillment), CRM, HR/payroll, project accounting, business intelligence, multi-currency, multi-entity consolidation. Target: 100-500 employee manufacturing/distribution companies.

## Key Requirements

### Finance Module
- General ledger with chart of accounts
- Accounts payable and receivable
- Fixed assets tracking
- Multi-currency transactions
- Multi-entity consolidation
- Bank reconciliation

### Inventory Module
- Multi-warehouse management
- Lot and serial number tracking
- Stock movements and adjustments
- Reorder points and par levels

### Procurement Module
- Purchase order management
- Vendor management and scoring
- Goods receipt and receiving
- Invoice matching

### Manufacturing Module
- Bill of Materials (BOM)
- Work order management
- Routing and production scheduling
- Quality control

### Sales Module
- Quotes and estimates
- Sales orders with fulfillment
- Customer management (CRM integration)
- Pricing and discounts

### HR Module
- Employee management
- Time and attendance
- Basic payroll processing

### Technical
- Role-based access control
- Audit trail
- REST API
- Report builder
- Dashboard analytics
