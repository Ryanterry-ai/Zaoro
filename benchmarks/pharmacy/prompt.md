# Pharmacy Benchmark Prompt

Build a comprehensive pharmacy management system for retail and specialty pharmacies. Features: prescription processing (eRx, fax, phone), inventory management (perpetual, controlled substances), insurance billing (NCPDP), compounding, medication synchronization, clinical services (MTM, immunizations), patient adherence, POS, delivery, compliance reporting (PDMP, DEA), multi-store, central fill, 340B, clinical decision support. Target: independent to mid-size pharmacy chains.

## Key Requirements

### Prescription Processing
- New prescription entry
- E-prescribing (Surescripts)
- Transfer handling
- Refill management

### Inventory
- Perpetual inventory
- Controlled substance tracking
- Lot/serial tracking
- 340B management

### Billing
- Insurance claims (NCPDP)
- COPAY handling
- DME billing
- Billing reconciliation

### Clinical
- MTM sessions
- Immunizations
- Adherence programs
- Drug interaction alerts

### Operations
- POS system
- Delivery management
- Multi-store support
- Central fill
